Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we protect and handle your data.

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: Meeting Minutes Bot is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered meeting assistant for Microsoft Teams.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Meeting Data

When you use Meeting Minutes Bot, we temporarily process:

  • Meeting Transcripts: Audio transcriptions provided by Microsoft Teams
  • Meeting Metadata: Meeting title, date, time, duration, and participant names
  • User Information: Names and email addresses of meeting participants
  • Teams Context: Team and channel information where the bot is installed

1.2 Usage Data

We collect limited usage information to improve our service:

  • Number of meetings processed
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Error logs and performance metrics
  • User preferences and settings

1.3 Account Information

  • Microsoft 365 account details (name, email, organization)
  • Subscription and billing information
  • Communication preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Use - AI Processing

We use your meeting data exclusively to:

  • Generate AI-powered meeting summaries and minutes
  • Identify and extract action items and decisions
  • Deliver meeting insights to authorized participants
  • Improve our AI models and service quality

2.2 Service Operations

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Meeting Minutes Bot
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Important: We do NOT use your meeting content for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to providing our service.

3. Data Storage and Retention

3.1 Temporary Processing

Meeting transcripts and audio data are:

  • Processed in real-time using secure AI infrastructure
  • Automatically deleted within 24 hours after processing
  • Never stored permanently on our servers
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols

3.2 Generated Content

AI-generated meeting minutes and summaries are:

  • Stored securely in Microsoft Teams (not on our servers)
  • Accessible only to authorized meeting participants
  • Subject to your organization's Microsoft 365 retention policies

3.3 Account Data

Account information and usage statistics are retained:

  • For the duration of your active subscription
  • Up to 90 days after account deletion (for legal compliance)
  • Anonymized analytics may be retained indefinitely

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 We Share Data With:

  • Microsoft Azure: Cloud infrastructure for hosting and processing
  • OpenAI: AI processing for generating meeting summaries (data is not used for model training)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe for billing (only payment information, not meeting data)
  • Analytics Services: Anonymized usage data only

4.2 We Do NOT Share:

  • Meeting content with advertisers or marketers
  • Personal information for third-party marketing
  • Data with unauthorized parties

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Protect user safety

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-leading security practices:

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Compliance: SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and Microsoft 365 security standards
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
  • Data Isolation: Tenant data is logically separated

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your generated meeting minutes
  • Opt-out: Disable the bot for specific meetings or channels

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at privacy@meetingminutesbot.com or through your account settings.

You can export all your meeting minutes in standard formats (PDF, JSON, Markdown) at any time.

7. International Data Transfers

Meeting Minutes Bot operates globally. Your data may be processed in:

  • United States (primary data centers)
  • European Union (for EU customers)
  • Other Microsoft Azure regions based on your organization's location

We ensure appropriate safeguards through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Microsoft's Data Protection Addendum
  • GDPR compliance for EU data

8. Children's Privacy

Meeting Minutes Bot is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

9. Cookies and Tracking

9.1 Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and service functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized usage statistics (can be disabled)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • In-app notification in Microsoft Teams
  • Notice on our website

Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

12. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Legal Basis: Legitimate interest and contractual necessity
  • Data Protection Officer: Available at dpo@meetingminutesbot.com
  • Right to Lodge Complaint: Contact your local data protection authority
  • Data Transfers: Protected by Standard Contractual Clauses

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests:

Email: privacy@meetingminutesbot.com

Data Protection Officer: dpo@meetingminutesbot.com

Support: support@meetingminutesbot.com

Effective Date: This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 5, 2026.
